COMPANY PROFILE

Our company was establish since 2003 by Ms Mais Al Kouzbari at Damascus , the capital of Syria ,as named

Al Kouzbari Co, for medical and Scientifics Equipments .
It is a leading distribution company in supplying Medical, Laboratory , Dental and scientific products from Multinational companies in the Healthcare industry. The company has in its portfolio over 40 products from medical supplies to hospital equipment and consumer health products .

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading company in the medical-health sector, recognized by institutions and society by the quality of our services and the Excellency of our professionals.

These Framework principals constitute the pillars which support our quality objective. They aim at ensuring continued satisfaction of our employees, customers and society in general.

Maintenance of internal policies that promote efficient management of our resources: human, material and financial.

  • Adapting and improving our facilities and equipment permanently to pursuit excellence in customer service.
  • An inspiration, a boost to our employee’s development by constantly improving their Knowledge and skills.
  • Implementing a quality management system that allows continuous improvement in the business processes.

The values that justify our services are

  • Recognition of human dignity, awareness of our customers’ rights together with the acknowledgement of personal talent, our great strength.
  • Our commitment to give responsible and personalized service to our stakeholders.
  • Continuing Education: providing training to employees to raise the level of knowledge and motivation in the organization.

Mission

Our mission is meeting the needs of the medical-health sector through the provision of medical ,health equipment , and instruments , ensuring the safety and welfare of patients all throughout GCC and ME.
