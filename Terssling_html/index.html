Hello, and welcome to Tressling

Thanks for stopping by to learn more about us. Tressling is about transforming businesses. Our Private Equity group transforms businesses by investing its time, capital, and experience to create larger and more profitable organizations. Our Management group transforms existing businesses by improving processes, organizational structures, information technologies, and the balance sheet. Our Partnership group works with international companies to transform their presence in international markets by opening new sales and distribution channels.

Tressling is growing, and growing strongly. Our private equity group is building on a track record of successful investments and making direct investments in a number of high potential businesses. The factory for our FastBuild construction system is well underway in Saudi Arabia, we have signed a deal for another in Bahrain, and we are working on plans for the UAE as well. We have launched our food and health products distribution business that will bring a number well established brands to the Middle East where the interest in improving one’s health and wellbeing is on the rise. Our Management group has signed a contract in Bahrain with a construction company, and our Partnerships group has entered into and is negotiating with a number of internationally recognized companies to expand and develop new distribution channels.

As I look ahead, I see vast opportunities and we are pursuing them with vigor. If you want your business to grow, to improve its processes, to develop new markets, to strengthen its balance sheet, I invite you to contact me to find out how Tressling can transform your business.

Dan McLaughlin
CEO & Founder
